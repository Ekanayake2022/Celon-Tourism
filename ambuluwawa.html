Ambuluwawa Tower

Ambuluwawa Tower, nestled in the lush greenery of Gampola, Sri Lanka, is a remarkable tourist attraction that combines natural beauty, spirituality, and breathtaking panoramic views.

Visitors can wander through the narrow cobblestone streets, explore ancient churches and museums, and immerse themselves in the enchanting atmosphere of the old town.

The tower is part of the Ambuluwawa Biodiversity Complex, which also encompasses beautifully landscaped gardens, meditation centers, and religious monuments.

Visitors can explore the complex, take a leisurely walk through the gardens, and soak in the serene atmosphere while enjoying the awe-inspiring vistas from the top of Ambuluwawa Tower.
